Intercept libraries such as Google Analytics, Mixpanel and KissMetrics.

Ana is the goto Analytics Interceptor that let's you see what websites are tracking about you.


  • Tell you which information websites are tracking about you.
  • Discards the tracking messages instead of sending to the analytics platform.
  • Lets you troubleshoot any issues with tracking if you are a web master.
